jUCMNav - Use Case Map Navigator

May 2005

jUCMNav was created as the capstone project for software engineering bachelor's degree at the University of Ottawa by Olivier Clift-Noel, Jean-Philippe Daigle, Jason Kealey, Jordan McManus, and Etienne Tremblay.

Developed under the supervision of Daniel Amyot, jUCMNav intends to replace UCMNav, the original Use Case Map navigator.

For more information about Use Case Maps, please visit usecasemaps.org.
